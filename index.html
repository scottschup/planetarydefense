Stargate: Asteroid Assault

The Goa'uld have sent a storm of asteroids hurtling toward Earth in an attempt to obliterate its inhabitants once and for all.

Atlantis, fending off its own Wraith attack, could only spare a single PuddleJumper to counter the Goa'uld assault.

You must blast the asteroids out of the sky before they can enter the atmosphere and wreak havoc on earth below.

Game Play and Controls

This is your ship: a PuddleJumper.
It can rotate clockwise (→) and counter-clockwise (←).
It can move propel itself forward (↑) and backward (↓).
It fires drones (space bar) and has a shield (shift key).

This is your enemy. Asteroids are headed for earth.
Stop them before they strike (the bottom of the screen)
to prevent people below from dying a terrible fiery death.

This is a drone. This is what you fire at the asteroids.
Drones are smart, they can detect targets and adjust course.
You have a limited supply of drones. Fire smartly rather than quickly.
You can refill your drone supply by going through Stargates.

Your ship is equipped with a shield.
It has a limited power supply.
You can recharge your shield by collecting ZPMs.

This is a Stargate.
Fly through the Stargate to refill your drone supply.

This is a Zero Point Module (ZPM).
Capture (fly into) the ZPM for more shield power.
